If $S$ is a stationary subset of $\kappa$ and $\lambda<\kappa$ implies $2^\lambda\leq\kappa$ then

$$\Diamond_S\leftrightarrow \clubsuit_S$$

Moreover, this is best possible: $\neg\Diamond_S$ is consistent with $\clubsuit_S$.
