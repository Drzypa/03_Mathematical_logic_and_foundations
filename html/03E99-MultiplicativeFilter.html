multiplicative filter


For any ring A, any set S⊂A and any element x∈A, we use the notation

(S:x):={a∈Aax∈S}

Let A be a commutative ring with unity, and let ℐ⁢(A) be the set of all ideals of A.

  • •

    A Multiplicative Filter of A is a filter ℱ on ℐ⁢(A) such that I,J∈ℱ⇒I⁢J∈ℱ.

  • •

    A Gabriel Filter of A is a filter ℱ on ℐ⁢(A) such that

    [I∈ℱ,J∈ℐ(A) and ∀x∈I,(J:x)∈ℱ]⇒J∈ℱ

Note that Gabriel Filters are also Multiplicative Filters.
